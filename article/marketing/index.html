Marketing Plan

The marketing plan outlines how we will attract users to the product.

It’s a plan that outlines what channels will be used (eg Paid Search, organic search, Facebook ads etc) as well expected numbers of visitors.

Sizing the audience

A way of doing this is to build an example landing page with an email capture field.

You outline the idea and then spend a small amount on Facebook or Google PPC and point people at the page. It’ll give you an idea of interest and a list or people to email about user research later.
