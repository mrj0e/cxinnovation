Opportunity statement

You have used the customer experience map and / or open research to identify an area to explore.

To take that further we need to develop an opportunity statement.

What is an opportunity statement

It’s statement that outlines the problem. It suggests we look at that problem in a different way. It is not a solution. Rather, it suggests more.

How might we

The statement starts with the phrase HOW MIGHT WE (abbr: HMW)

How might we offer a current account that meets the needs of our younger customers?

How might we provide credit reference information in the future?

How might we encourage children to exercise more?

How might we meet the needs of hotel guests in China?

Bad examples:

How might we use the Apple Watch to to transform savings accounts?

It contains a solution, the watch

How might we develop a market leading current account?

We are framing the problem in terms of the competition not what we want to achieve.

Format

This can be run in a 1/2 day workshop.
