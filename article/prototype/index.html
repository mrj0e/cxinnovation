Prototyping the idea

The prototype is used to show the idea. A tool to show how the final product will work. Something to get the organisation excited about the project.

How to do it

We can build prototypes no problem here at cxpartners. There a number of ways to do it, all discussed in a Google Doc.

Towards a process and tools for prototyping

You can use Mixture app and the Foundation framework to make your prototype, that's what this guide is built on.

Quick research
