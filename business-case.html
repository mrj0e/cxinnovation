Developing a credible business case

The business case is financial planning and organisational work needed to be done before launch. More specifically it is a breakdown of the costs and profits for the planned product.

The business case is presented to the board of directors to ask for the funding to develop the product. Remember the work we are doing here is develop the idea not build the idea.

cxpartners will normally not be responsible for the final production of the business plan but we will input and heavily influence it’s content.

How cxpartners get involved

We’ve developed a Business Innovation Canvas which shows all the elements the business plan needs to cover.

We help analyse Metrics and set KPIs

Inputs

The business case needs the following inputs (this is taken from Business plans for Dummies, I kid you not)

  • Describe the business.

  • Describe the product or service.

  • Describe the competitive environment of the product.

  • Describe how the business will make money through a business model.

  • Describe how to market the business.

  • Describe how you’re going to make / build the product.

  • Describe the business team.

  • Describe the financial projection, including how much money you need and how much money you will profit.
